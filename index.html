Full Terminal Support

$ gni create new zombie sound effect
Created! This was added to your session list.
  Type 'gni play' to hear the sound effect.
  Type 'gni regen' to try generating one again.
  Type 'gni checkout' to download your session list
  Type 'gni delete' to remove this file from your session list.

$ gni play


$ gni regen
Regenerated!

$ gni play


$ gni checkout --zip --clipboard
Zipped your session list and copied to clipboard!
